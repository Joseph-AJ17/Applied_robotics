# Activity 1: Examples of robots by morphology

## Objective:
Investigate and show examples of real life examples of robots with different morphologies.

## Types of robots based on its morphology

### 1.- Cartesian Robot (XYZ or Gantry)

**Morphology and motion**

- Moves along three orthogonal linear axes (X, Y, Z).
- No primary rotational joints.
- Simple kinematics, high rigidity, and high positional accuracy.

<img src="../recursos/imgs/Task_1/Example_cartesian.png" alt=" Cartesian Robot" width="300" allign=center>

**Typical applications**

- CNC machines
- 3D printing
- Pick & place systems
- Material handling

### 2.- Cylindrical Robot

**Morphology and motion**

Cylindrical work envelope.

Functions by combining:

- 1 rotational joint (base rotation).

- 2 linear motions (radial and vertical).

    <img src="../recursos/imgs/Task_1/Example_cylindrical.png" alt=" Cylindrical Robot" width="300" allign=center>

**Typical applications**

- Machine loading/unloading.

- Simple assembly.

- Material transfer.

**Real-world examples**

- Cylindrical Automation Products

    <img src="../recursos/imgs/Task_1/Cylindrical_Automation_Products.jpg" alt="Cylindrical Automation Products" width="300" allign=center>

### 3.- Polar Robot (Spherical Robot)

**Morphology and motion**

Spherical work envelope.

Combines:

- 2 rotational joints.

- 1 linear joint.

    <img src="../recursos/imgs/Task_1/Example_spherical.png" alt="Polar Robot (Spherical Robot)" width="300" allign=center>

**Typical applications**

- Welding.

- Die casting.

- Handling of large components.

### 4.- Articulated Robot (Jointed-Arm Robot)

Act almost like if it was a human arm.

**Morphology and motion**

- Composed entirely of rotational joints, this helps to have a similar human arm motion.

- Typically 6 degrees of freedom (6-DOF).

- High flexibility and complex reach.

    <img src="../recursos/imgs/Task_1/Example_arm.png" alt="Articulated Robot (Jointed-Arm Robot)" width="300" allign=center>

**Typical applications**

- Arc and spot welding.

- Painting.

- Complex assembly.

- Collaborative robotics.

**Real-world examples**

- KUKA KR 210

    <img src="../recursos/imgs/Task_1/KUKA_KR_210.jpg" alt="KUKA KR 210" width="300" allign=center>

- FANUC M-20iA 20M

    <img src="../recursos/imgs/Task_1/si.jpg" alt="FANUC M-20iA 20M" width="300" allign=center>
